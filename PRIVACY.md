# Privacy Policy for Nine Star Ki

**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025

## Introduction

Nine Star Ki ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the Nine Star Ki mobile application.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The Nine Star Ki app:
- Does NOT collect your birth date or any personal information
- Does NOT store any data on our servers
- Does NOT track your usage or behavior
- Does NOT use analytics or telemetry services
- Does NOT share data with third parties

## How the App Works

All calculations are performed **locally on your device only**:
- Birth dates you enter are processed entirely within the app on your device
- Your Nine Star Ki profile is calculated using local algorithms
- No data is sent to external servers or stored permanently
- When you close the app, no trace of your information remains

## Third-Party Services

The app contains a single link to Make Space For More (https://www.makespaceformore.com) for educational resources. Clicking this link opens your device's web browser and is subject to that website's privacy policy.

## Permissions

The app does not request any device permissions such as:
- Camera
- Location
- Contacts
- Photos
- Microphone
- Or any other sensitive permissions

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
